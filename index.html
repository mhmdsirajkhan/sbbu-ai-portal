SBBU AI Usage Framework

Follow these color-coded rules for all academic assignments.

Green: Permitted

  • Brainstorming research topics.
  • Creating essay outlines.
  • Explaining complex concepts.

No Disclosure Required

Yellow: Acknowledge

  • Drafting specific sections.
  • Translating academic texts.
  • Grammar and style checking.

Must include Disclosure

Red: Prohibited

  • Generating full research papers.
  • Solving online exam questions.
  • Copy-pasting AI text directly.

Violates Academic Integrity
